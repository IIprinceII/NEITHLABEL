Privacy Policy — NEITHLABEL Pinterest Integration

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This application (the “App”) is a private tool used by NEITHLABEL to manage our own Pinterest business account.

1. Information We Access

The App uses the Pinterest API to read and write content (pins, boards, analytics) only on the Pinterest account that has explicitly authorized it — i.e., the account owner of NEITHLABEL. We do not access data belonging to any other user.

2. Information We Store

The App stores OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by Pinterest, solely to maintain the authorized session. Tokens are stored locally and are not shared with third parties. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

3. How We Use Information

  • Publishing and scheduling pins to our own boards
  • Reading analytics for our own pins and boards
  • Organizing our own content

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any Pinterest data to third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are integrated.

5. Data Retention

Tokens are retained until revoked by the account owner or by Pinterest. Revoking access through Pinterest settings immediately disables the App.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
