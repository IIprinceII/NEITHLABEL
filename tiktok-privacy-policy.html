Privacy Policy — NEITHLABEL TikTok Integration

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This application (the “App”) is a private tool used by NEITHLABEL to publish content to our own TikTok account using the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Information We Access

The App uses the TikTok API to upload and publish videos only on the TikTok account that has explicitly authorized it — i.e., the account owner of NEITHLABEL. We do not access data belonging to any other user.

2. Information We Store

The App stores OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok, solely to maintain the authorized session. Tokens are stored locally (or as encrypted CI secrets) and are not shared with third parties. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.

3. How We Use Information

  • Uploading and scheduling videos to our own TikTok account
  • Reading the publish status of videos we have submitted
  • Organizing our own content queue

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any TikTok data to third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are integrated.

5. Data Retention

Tokens are retained until revoked by the account owner or by TikTok. Revoking access through TikTok settings immediately disables the App.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
